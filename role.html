Augustinian Student Government

Mission

Guided by the Augustinian core values, the Augustinian Student Government is committed to unwaveringly advocating for the well-being and interests of the Augustinian student community. Through principled leadership, service-oriented initiatives, and transparent governance, the Augustinian Student Government aims to foster an atmosphere of inclusivity, mutual respect, and cooperation, thereby leaving behind an enduring legacy of constructive transformation.

Vision

The Augustinian Student Government strives to become a beacon of student leadership within the Senior High School Department of Colegio San Agustin – Biñan, nurturing a lively community where each student is empowered to flourish, actively participate, and achieve excellence.

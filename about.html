Augustinian Student Government


History

The Augustinian Student Government (ASG) has been an integral part of the Senior High School Department at Colegio San Agustin – Biñan since its establishment in 2016. From its inception to its present state, the ASG has been instrumental in advocating for student interests, fostering community engagement, and promoting student leadership.


Foundation

In 2016, the ASG was formally established with the aim of representing the diverse voices and interests of the Senior High School student body. Its formation was a response to the need for a unified platform to address student concerns and facilitate positive change within the school community.

Early Years

During its initial years, the ASG focused on building its infrastructure, establishing its constitution, and defining its mission and objectives. It laid the groundwork for future initiatives and solidified its role as the official representative body for Senior High School students.

Milestones and Achievements

Over the years, the ASG has achieved significant milestones and made notable contributions to campus life. One of the most notable events hosted by the ASG is Kislap. Project Kislap is a big annual event held in December, aimed at raising funds for upcoming events, programs, and charitable causes, particularly for an outreach program entitled Project TY. Project Kislap also aims to provide year-end entertainment, giving audiences a fun, enjoyable, and memorable experience.

Leadership, Service, and Transparency

The ASG espouses the core values of Leadership, Service, and Transparency, which are consistently upheld in all its endeavors. These values guide the ASG in its commitment to serving as a model of leadership, engaging in selfless service to the student body, and maintaining transparency in its actions and decisions.


The Augustinian Student Government Today

Today, the ASG stands as a symbol of student empowerment, collaboration, and advocacy. It remains committed to its founding principles while continuously striving to address the evolving needs and aspirations of the Senior High School student body.

Augustinian Student Government

Overview

The Augustinian Student Government (ASG) leads Colegio San Agustin – Biñan's Senior High School, advocating for students and fostering leadership since 2016. They value leadership, service and transparency, running events like Project Kislap to raise funds and create a strong school community.
